The Arm Bar

Juji Gatame

Execute from mount.

  1. Hold their forearm so their wrist is facing upwards
  2. Position your knees bent over your partner, one leg over their neck and the other over their waist
  3. Squeeze your legs and pull their arm towards you
  4. Raise your hips up, creating a fulcrum as you pull their wrist twoards your chest in the direction of their pinky finger
